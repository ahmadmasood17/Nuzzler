About Us

How It Started

It all started when my dog gave me a quick little nuzzle with his nose...his way of saying, hey...you’re my person...I love you! A simple gesture like that...it can hold tons of feeling and make you feel good in an instant.

just Nuzzle was born out of that moment. It’s a simple idea...to help people connect with those they love in a quick yet meaningful way...to share the moments that are special to us, with the people that are special to us.

That little Nuzzle from my dog taught me how easy it can be to show that you care. So let someone know that you’re thinking of them…send them a Nuzzle today!

Brian Mahoney
Founder - Nuzzler

Why Choose Nuzzler?

We believe that human connection is the most important part of life. Whether near or far, a Nuzzle is the perfect gift to show your love, appreciation, and thoughtfulness in a unique and personalized way.

With Nuzzler, connection grows stronger and hearts feel brighter...once meaningful video at a time.

Join the Nuzzler Community

By joining Nuzzler, you become part of a growing community that values connection, love, & thoughtfulness. Sending a Nuzzle is more than just a video—it's a meaningful way to brighten someone’s day and stay close to the people who matter most.

Whether you’re creating a message for a loved one, a friend, or even colleagues, Nuzzler helps you express what’s in your heart.
